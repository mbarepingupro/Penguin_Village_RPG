Manual operator tools -- nothing here fires automatically, every action below is a button you click yourself. None of these touch player gold, gear, resources, XP, level, achievements, or titles -- only building level/donation tracking and leaderboard/milestone- progress records.
🏗️ BUILDING RESET
Resets level to 1 and all donation totals to 0 for the selected building, and clears its per-building 100-donated card-background milestone. Only 5 of the 11 buildings are actually donation-upgradeable (Sea Lion Pit, Club Soda, Parkmusement, Cursed Temple, Guillotine) -- resetting any other building just clears its card-background milestone, if it has one (Hotel does).
🖼️ MILESTONES RESET
Resets contribution-milestone PROGRESS only -- cosmetic frames/ backgrounds a player already unlocked are gear rows and stay with them (out of scope here), same as achievements/titles.
🏆 LEADERBOARD RESET
Clears ranking/score records only -- rewards already paid out (N00Tboxes, resources, gold) stay with players either way.
